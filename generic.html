I began my professional journey in 2018, right after earning my bachelor's, joining ATOS - a leading European IT services and consulting firm.

Building Smarter Data Pipelines at ATOS

At ATOS, I spearheaded initiatives that automated workflows, optimized data pipelines, and enabled real-time business insights. Key achievements include:

  • ✅ Reduced manual reporting workload by 40% by automating Power BI dashboards, enhancing real-time data accessibility.
  • ✅ Optimized SQL-based ETL pipelines, cutting data processing time by 80%, accelerating decision-making.
  • ✅ Developed Python scripts to automate Oracle data retrieval, slashing manual efforts by 50% and boosting efficiency.
  • ✅ Created 25+ interactive Tableau dashboards, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions 40% faster.

My expertise in SQL, Python, Tableau, and ETL workflows transformed complex datasets into powerful insights, significantly reducing overhead and improving operational efficiency.

Driving Data Integration & Intelligence at On Call International (OCI)

As part of my graduate capstone project at NYU Tandon, I collaborated with OCI, a Tokio Marine company, known for its global travel risk management and emergency assistance solutions.

  • ✅ Unified and integrated OCI's decentralized GOP facility data into their Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM platform to enhance service response efficiency and operational intelligence.
  • ✅ Collaborated closely with stakeholders through regular meetings to understand requirements, map existing workflows, identify process gaps, and translate insights into actionable data integration strategies.
  • ✅ Cleaned and standardized provider data by resolving address inconsistencies and mismatched country names using a mix of Python web scraping. Increased data records by almost 50%.
  • ✅ Enabled accurate facility mapping by validating address data through geolocation APIs and integrating enriched records into Microsoft Dynamics 365, with location visualization via Azure Maps.
  • ✅ Maintained alignment and transparency by managing deliverables and milestones in JIRA, and creating detailed documentation covering data formats, integration architecture, and synchronization logic.
